UK DELIVERIES

All UK orders will be shipped with a UPS Next Day Delivery service.

UPS Next Day Delivery - £4

UPS Next Day Delivery free on orders over £250

Orders placed Monday to Thursday before 12pm will be dispatched the same day.

Orders placed on Fridays will not be processed until Monday, as we operate a 4 day working week at the Lavenham factory.

In the interest of our factory team's wellbeing as well as conserving energy, the Lavenham factory have moved to a four day working week. This means that orders placed after 12pm on a Thursday will not be dispatched until Monday.

INTERNATIONAL DELIVERIES

The exact shipping methods available for your country will be presented at checkout. Duties and taxes will be automatically calculated and displayed at checkout.

*Please contact customerservice@lavenhamjackets.com if you require any further information.

You can return items within 28 days of receipt for a refund of that item or exchange it to a different size or colour of the same item, depending on stock. Please ensure the original labels are attached to the garment.

UK RETURNS AND EXCHANGES

You can return items within 28 days of receipt for a refund of that item or exchange it to a different size or colour of the same item, depending on stock.

If you would like to exchange, please email or ring and we will put your required size/colour to one side for you.

Please ensure the original labels are attached to the garment.

Please send the order to the below address with a returns form stating your order number.

Lavenham Returns 24-25 Churchfield Road Sudbury Suffolk CO10 2YA

INTERNATIONAL RETURNS AND EXCHANGES

You can return items within 28 days of receipt for a refund of that item or exchange it to a different size or colour of the same item, depending on stock. Please ensure the original labels are attached to the garment.

If you would like to return or exchange please contact our customer service team [customerservice@lavenhamjackets.com] and we can send you a pre-paid returns label, the cost of which will be deducted from your refund.

Pre-paid returns are subject to a return shipping fee which will be deducted from your refund.

Alternatively you can send the order back to us using the shipper of your choice at your expense.

Please note that duties, taxes and shipping cost are non-refundable.
