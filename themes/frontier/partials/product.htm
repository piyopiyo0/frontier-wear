Description

A collaboration with London brand YMC who have been referencing military and workwear with a focus on fabric and fits since 1995.

With an oversized fit this jacket features a ribbed collar, cuffs and waistband and two large front pockets. Quilted in a military pattern with a lightweight yet warm wadding, it is finished with a zip fastening.

Outer: 100% Recycled polyester
Wadding: Partially recycled polyester wadding
